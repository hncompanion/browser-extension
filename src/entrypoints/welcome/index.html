Welcome to Hacker News Companion

Enhance your Hacker News experience with AI summaries, keyboard navigation, and more.

Key Features

AI-Powered Summaries

Get concise summaries of lengthy discussions. Use cached summaries from our server or generate fresh ones with your preferred LLM provider (Google, OpenAI, Anthropic, Ollama, and more).

Vim-Style Keyboard Navigation

Navigate stories and comments efficiently with familiar keybindings. Use j/k to move between items, ? to view all shortcuts.

User Hover Previews

Hover over any username to see their karma and about info without leaving the page. Quickly assess commenter credibility and history.

Getting Started

The extension works out of the box with cached summaries from our server. To generate fresh summaries with your own API keys, configure your preferred LLM provider in settings.

Go to Hacker News Configure Settings

Press ? on any Hacker News page to view keyboard shortcuts.
